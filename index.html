FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What types of video can I upload? +
Any long-form video works — coaching sessions, podcasts, webinars, YouTube videos, Zoom recordings, or interviews. We support MP4, MOV, and most common formats up to 2 hours in length.
How long does it take to get my clips? +
Most videos are processed and your clips are ready within 2–5 minutes. Longer videos (1 hour+) may take up to 10 minutes. You'll get a notification when they're ready to download.
Can I customise the captions and branding? +
Yes! On Growth and Scale plans you can add your logo, choose caption font and colors, and apply brand presets. You can also manually edit captions before downloading if needed.
Which platforms are the clips optimised for? +
All clips are exported in 9:16 vertical format at 1080×1920 resolution — perfect for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn, and Twitter/X video.
Can I cancel at any time? +
Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. Cancel your subscription any time from your account settings — you'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
How do I pay? +
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe, as well as Apple Pay and Google Pay. Email us to get started and we'll send a secure payment link.
